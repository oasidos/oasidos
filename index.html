About Us

Quality & Craftsmanship From Shenzhen

Founded and based in Shenzhen, the global manufacturing hub, OASIDOS was born out of a passion for outdoor exploration and reliable technical accessories. We understand what outdoor enthusiasts, overlanders and remote workers truly need.

We specialize in designing, developing and producing full series of Starlink Mini accessories. From protective cases and mounting brackets to tripods and vehicle adapters, every item is engineered for long-term use in all weather conditions.

Strict quality control runs through every production process. We stick to solid materials, precise workmanship and user-friendly design, to deliver products you can trust wherever your journey takes you.
